FAVOURITE CITY


LAHORE (CITY OF GARDENS)

Lahore is the second biggest city in Pakistan. It is the capital of the province of Punjab. It is also known as the 'City of Gardens' because of its many parks and gardens. This city is known for its rich culture and lively atmosphere.


ISLAMABAD (THE GREEN CITY)

It is famous for its overall greenery, peace and cleanliness. The places worth seeing in this city include Lok Virsa Museum, Rawal Lake, Faisal Mosque, Pir Sohawa, Islamabad Zoo, Pakistan Museum of National History, the Centaurus Mall, etc.


PESHAWAR (CITY OF FLOWERS)

There was a time when Peshawar was known as the city of flowers. Perhaps this was because of the many gardens built in the 16th and 17th centuries during the Mughal Era.


FAISLABAD (THE MANCHESTER OF PAKISTAN)

The reason Faisalabad is called Manchester of Pakistan because it's the biggest textile city of Pakistan and all the industries here related to textiles and same is Manchester in UK so these cities are also named as Twin cities. After Lahore, Faisalabad is famous for its food.
